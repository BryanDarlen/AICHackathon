# AI Marathon 2026 Problem Statements

The AI Marathon 2026 problem statements are live. Teams should choose one track, define their agent architecture, and start building a practical prototype.

## Track 1: The Autonomous Sales Engineer

### Challenge

Complex sales are rarely about finding one item. They are about building a complete system where all parts are compatible, fit the client's budget, and can be delivered to the client's specific location.

### Mission

Build an agent that acts as a **Technical Sales Consultant**. The agent must take a high-level requirements brief from a client and autonomously navigate a product catalog to design a complete, valid, and quoted solution.

### Expected Capabilities

- Interpret client requirements.
- Search and reason across a product catalog.
- Check compatibility between selected components.
- Consider budget and delivery constraints.
- Produce a complete recommended solution with a quotation.

## Track 2: The Intelligent Recruiter

### Challenge

Traditional job boards are static. They often fail to bridge the gap between diverse talent and hiring managers who need fast, relevant candidate shortlists.

### Mission

Create an agent that takes a job description and a pool of candidate data, such as resumes or profiles, and identifies the best matches.

### Expected Capabilities

- Parse job descriptions and candidate profiles.
- Match skills, experience, and role requirements.
- Rank candidates with clear reasoning.
- Highlight gaps, risks, and strengths.
- Produce recruiter-friendly recommendations.

## Track 3: The Global Treasury Agent

### Challenge

SMEs often struggle with cross-border reconciliation. For example, a company may receive RM42.50 into its bank account while the invoice was issued for USD 10. Manual matching requires currency conversion, calculation, and logging.

### Mission

Build a reconciliation agent that processes payment proofs, such as images, PDFs, or Excel files, and matches them against local bank statements across multiple currencies.

### Expected Capabilities

- Extract payment information from images, PDFs, or spreadsheets.
- Interpret invoices, bank statements, and proof of payment.
- Convert and compare values across currencies.
- Match transactions against bank records.
- Flag mismatches and generate reconciliation logs.

## Team Guidance

- Discuss the tracks with your team before choosing.
- Pick the challenge where your team can build the strongest agent workflow.
- Plan the tools, data flow, validation checks, and user experience early.
- Focus on producing a working, explainable prototype.

Good luck, and build something useful.
